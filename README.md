# Super Shop Dashboard

An interactive **Super Shop Dashboard** built with **Streamlit**. This dashboard allows users to explore sales and product data easily with interactive charts and tables.  

---

## **Features**

1. **Overview**  
   - Preview dataset  
   - Key metrics: Total Sales, Total Quantity, Average Unit Price  
   - Line chart of sales trend  

2. **Product Analysis**  
   - Bar chart of sales by product  
   - Interactive filtering by product  

3. **City Analysis**  
   - Pie chart of sales share by city  
   - Compare sales across different cities
